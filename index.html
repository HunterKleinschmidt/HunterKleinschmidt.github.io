How I Work

My Analytical Approach

As an analyst at heart, I bring a structured, analytical mindset to every project—whether it’s data analysis, project management, or strategic decision-making. My 6-step process ensures clarity, insight, and impact, tailored to the unique needs of each challenge.

1. Clarify the Vision

I begin by deeply understanding the project’s goals, asking critical questions to define the scope and objectives—whether it’s solving a data problem, launching a product, or managing a team initiative.

2. Gather Information

I collect the necessary information to inform the project, whether it’s data from GA4 and Firebase, project metrics, or business context, ensuring a solid foundation for analysis or decision-making.

3. Structure the Approach

I organize the information or project components analytically, creating scalable data pipelines with SQL and Python or structured workflows to ensure efficiency and clarity.

4. Uncover Insights

I dive deep to find patterns and solutions, using tools like R and Excel for data analysis or applying analytical frameworks to project challenges, ensuring actionable outcomes.

5. Communicate Clearly

I present insights or project progress with clarity, using visualizations in Tableau or GA4 for data projects, and detailed reports or presentations for stakeholders, ensuring alignment and understanding.

6. Drive Impact

I turn insights into action, whether it’s optimizing a product strategy with data, improving project workflows, or guiding strategic decisions, delivering measurable results.
